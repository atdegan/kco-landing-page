Kettle & Co. is a startup Co-founders Katie and Allison had a dream. One where people could access tea easier, more efficiently, and more elegantly. At Kettle & Co., we want to created the world’s first streamlined electric kettle. By incorporating high-tech function with a classic, elegant look, Kettle & Co. will bring you a kettle that you can display with pride but also use effortlessly to make the perfect cup of tea. We will be employing a temperature-control function with a keep-warm feature and a noise element to let you know when your water is ready. These kettles will look good in any kitchen. From SoHo lofts to Brooklyn brownstones and New England colonials to Texas ranch houses, these kettles will add a unique and aesthetic feature to your kitchens and tea-stations.
Statistic Brain.com states that 2 billion Americans drink tea every morning, with $15 billion in sales grossed in 2012. We think it’s safe to say Americans love tea, and it’s time we’ve made it our own. Kettle & Co.’s vision is to provide every tea-drinker in America with a reliable and aesthetic piece of equipment that enriches their daily routine. Classic. Sleek. Bright. That’s what we are, and we can’t wait to get steeping.
